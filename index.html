Tianqi Jiang

Biography

My name is Tianqi Jiang, and I am a graduate student at Shandong university of science and technology, China, where I major in mining engineering. My current research direction is “long term strength and stability of coal pillar” and “waste processing and its usage in coal mine backfill materials”.

Interests

  • Rock mechanics
  • Mine backfilling materials
  • Concrete materials
  • Building materials
  • Long-term stability of concrete and its structure
  • Solid waste treatment
  • Coal mining
  • Failure prediction of rock under long term coupling effect of water, heat and mechanics via acoustic emission
